# /business-plan - Business Planning Assistant

Generate a complete **Business Plan** for your venture.

## Usage

```bash
/business-plan "digital marketing agency"
/business-plan "food delivery service"
/business-plan "SaaS startup"
```

## Output Includes

- Executive summary
- Market analysis
- Revenue model
- Competitive landscape
- Financial projections
- Operational plan

## Best Practices

1. **Be specific** about your target market
2. **Include realistic** financial projections
3. **Update regularly** as your business evolves

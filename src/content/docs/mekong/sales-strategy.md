# /sales-strategy - Sales Playbook Generator

Create comprehensive **Sales Strategies** tailored to your market and offerings.

## Usage

```bash
/sales-strategy "SaaS product launch"
/sales-strategy "local service business"
/sales-strategy "e-commerce store"
```

## Agents Spawned

- **Strategist** - Market positioning and sales approach
- **Researcher** - Competitor and pricing analysis

## Output Includes

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Positioning** | How to differentiate |
| **Pricing** | Strategy and tiers |
| **Channels** | Where to sell |
| **Playbook** | Step-by-step sales process |

# /growth-strategy - Scaling Your Business

Develop a comprehensive **Growth Strategy** for sustainable expansion.

## Usage

```bash
/growth-strategy "scale from $10k to $100k MRR"
/growth-strategy "expand to new markets"
/growth-strategy "product-led growth"
```

## Output Includes

- Growth objectives and timeline
- Acquisition channels
- Retention strategies
- Team scaling plan
- Financial projections

## Best Practices

1. **Focus on retention** - Growth without retention is a leaky bucket
2. **Double down on what works** - Find your growth engine
3. **Invest in systems** - Scale processes, not just people

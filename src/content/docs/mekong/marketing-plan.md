# /marketing-plan - Marketing Strategy Builder

Generate a complete **Marketing Plan** tailored to your business and market.

## Usage

```bash
/marketing-plan "e-commerce fashion brand"
/marketing-plan "local restaurant chain"
/marketing-plan "B2B SaaS platform"
```

## Output Includes

- Target audience analysis
- Channel strategy (SEO, Paid, Social)
- Content calendar framework
- Budget allocation recommendations
- KPIs and measurement plan

## Best Practices

1. **Define clear objectives** - What success looks like
2. **Know your budget** - Be realistic about resources
3. **Test and iterate** - Marketing is ongoing optimization

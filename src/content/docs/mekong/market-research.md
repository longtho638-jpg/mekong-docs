# /market-research - Competitive Intelligence

Conduct comprehensive **Market Research** for your business.

## Usage

```bash
/market-research "food delivery apps"
/market-research "digital marketing agencies"
/market-research "subscription box services"
```

## Output Includes

- Market size and trends
- Competitor analysis
- Opportunity gaps
- Target audience insights
- Pricing benchmarks

## Best Practices

1. **Be specific** about your niche
2. **Update quarterly** - Markets change fast
3. **Act on insights** - Research without action is waste
